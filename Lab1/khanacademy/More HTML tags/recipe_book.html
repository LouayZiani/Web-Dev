Recipe #1: Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti + pancetta or Bacon + Eggs
+ Parmesan Cheese + Garlic+ salt&pepper 		400g + 150g+ 4
+ 100g + 2 cloves

Steps

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling
Step 2: Fry pancetta with garlic until crispy, then remove garlic
Step 3: Beat eggs with Parmesan in a bowl
Step 4: Drain pasta, add to pancetta, remove from heat, stir in egg-cheese mixture

Recipe #2: Chocolate chip cookies

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter + Brown Sugar + Eggs + Vanilla + Flour
+Baking soda + Salt + Chocolate chips 		200g + 150g + 100g+ 2 + 1 tbsp + 300g +
+ 1 tbsp + 0.5 tbsp + 200g

Steps

Step 1: Cream butter and sugars together until fluffy
Step 2: Beat in eggs and vanilla
Step 3: Mix flour, baking soda, and salt; add to wet mixture
Step 4: Stir in chocolate chips
Step 5: Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet, bake at 180°C for 10–12 minutes

Recipe #3: French toast

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
bread slices + Eggs + milk + sugar
+cinnamon + butter + maple syrup 		4 + 2 + 0.5 cup + 1 tbsp
+ 0.5 tbsp + 2 tbsp

Steps

Step 1: Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl
Step 2: Dip bread slices into the mixture until soaked
Step 3: Melt butter in a pan over medium heat
Step 4: Cook bread slices until golden brown on both sides
Step 5: Serve warm with maple syrup
